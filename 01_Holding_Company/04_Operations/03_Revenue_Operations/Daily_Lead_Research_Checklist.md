# 1. Purpose

A short, repeatable daily checklist for lead research during the Revenue Launch.

---

# 2. Daily Checklist

| Step | Task | Target |
|------|------|--------|
| 1 | Time-box a research session | 30-45 minutes |
| 2 | Find new leads from approved sources (RLOP-002) | 3-5 leads |
| 3 | Qualify each new lead against STRAT-008 scoring | All new leads scored |
| 4 | Log qualified leads into `Lead_Tracker.csv` | Every qualifying lead logged |
| 5 | Flag any lead needing CEO approval before contact | Marked in tracker |
| 6 | Write one summary line for Atlas's daily briefing | 1 line |

---

# 3. Daily Summary Line Format

```text
Lead research: [X] found, [Y] qualified, [Z] flagged for CEO approval. Total pool: [N].
```

This line feeds into `Atlas_CEO_Command_Center.md`'s lead tracker summary.
