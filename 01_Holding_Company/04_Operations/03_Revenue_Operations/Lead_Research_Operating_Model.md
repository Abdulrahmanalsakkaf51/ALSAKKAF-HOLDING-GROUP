# 1. Purpose

This document defines how ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP finds and qualifies leads for AOS AI Services during the Revenue Launch. It is the operating model behind `Lead_Tracker.csv`, `First_25_Leads_Research_Instructions.md` (RLOP-002), and the Daily Lead Research Checklist (RLOP-003).

---

# 2. Research Method — Manual Only

At this stage, all lead research is **manual and human-driven**. No scraping, no automated data collection, no browser automation, and no network access by Claude or Atlas without a separate, explicit CEO approval. A human (the Founder, or a future CEO-approved research action) reads public information and records it by hand.

---

# 3. Lead Source Categories

| Source | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Personal network / referrals | People the Founder already knows who run or work at a small business |
| Public business directories | Chamber of commerce listings, local business association directories, public "find a business" pages |
| LinkedIn (manual browsing) | A human reads public profiles/company pages — no scraping tool, no automated connection requests |
| Local business associations | SME associations, coworking spaces, startup communities |
| Existing AOS content audience | Once content (STRAT-010) generates any inbound interest, those contacts become leads |

---

# 4. Qualification Approach

Leads are qualified using the scoring model defined in `AOS_Client_Lead_Pipeline_and_Outreach_System.md` (STRAT-008): fit, budget signal, urgency, and reachability, each scored and combined into a priority tier. A lead is not logged as "Qualified" in `Lead_Tracker.csv` until it has been scored against that model.

---

# 5. Atlas's Role — What Atlas Does and Does Not Do

Atlas prepares and maintains this system: the tracker structure, the research instructions, the daily checklist, and the report template. Atlas may also help score and organize leads once a human has found and entered them.

Atlas does **not** go out and find real leads on its own authority. Atlas has no network access to browse directories, LinkedIn, or any website unless the Founder has given separate, explicit approval for a specific research action. Until then, `Lead_Tracker.csv` stays empty except for its template/example row.

---

# 6. Logging Discipline

Every lead entered into `Lead_Tracker.csv` must have, at minimum: Company Name, a real public Website or Contact Page (if available), an honest Problem Observed note, and a Lead Score. No lead is entered without a genuine, verifiable public source — never invented or guessed information.
