# 1. Purpose

The template Atlas uses to summarize a lead research session for the Founder, feeding into the Atlas CEO Command Center (ACC-001).

---

# 2. Template

```text
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]
Leads Found: [N]
Leads Qualified: [N]
Top 3 Leads:
1. [Company] — Score [N] — [one-line reason]
2. [Company] — Score [N] — [one-line reason]
3. [Company] — Score [N] — [one-line reason]
Patterns Observed: [e.g. common industry, common problem type]
Recommended Next Action: [e.g. "Ready for CEO outreach approval" or "Continue research"]
```

---

# 3. Example (Current State — No Research Yet)

```text
Date: 2026-07-13
Leads Found: 0
Leads Qualified: 0

Recommended Next Action: Begin Day 9 of the 14-Day Revenue Sprint (STRAT-006) — first manual lead research session
```
