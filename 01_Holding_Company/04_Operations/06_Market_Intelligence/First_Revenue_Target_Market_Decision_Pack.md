# 1. Purpose

Help the Founder choose ONE first target market for the revenue sprint. Thirteen candidates are scored honestly below. No real company names appear here — leads are researched individually on Day 2.

Scoring: each market gets a priority score out of 10, weighing (a) how real and frequent the pain is, (b) ability and willingness to pay $399-450 USD, (c) how reachable the decision maker is for a solo founder with email/DM, (d) delivery simplicity within our no-coding scope, and (e) payment friction (our only active rail is PayPal in USD).

## 3.1 USA Solo Consultants / Coaches — Score 9

| Field | Assessment |
|-------|------------|

| Why they need AOS | They ARE the business — a Manager/Research/Operations agent trio directly multiplies their only worker |
| Offer fit | $450 AI Agent Pack (primary), $399 as alternate |

# 4. Recommendation

Start with **USA solo consultants / coaches** (score 9): highest offer fit for the new $450 AI Agent Pack, native PayPal/USD payment, decision-maker reachable directly, English-only delivery, zero compliance surface.
