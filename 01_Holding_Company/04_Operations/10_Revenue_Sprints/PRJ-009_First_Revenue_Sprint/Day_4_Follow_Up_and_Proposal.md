# 1. Morning — Handle Replies First (up to 60 min)

For every reply received:

- [ ] Interested → offer a short call (use the discovery script AIAT-005) or answer their question directly, same day
- [ ] Question → answer honestly within the no-hype rules; no guarantees
- [ ] No / not now → thank them genuinely, log Reply Status, mark for a future cycle
- [ ] Update `Outreach_Tracker.csv` and `Client_Pipeline.csv` for anything that moved

# 2. Research 5 More Leads (60 min)

Keep the pipeline breathing: add 5 new leads to `Lead_Tracker.csv` using the Day 2 method. (Every non-reply day adds leads.)

# 3. Prepare Your First Proposal (90 min)

Even without a warm reply yet, build the proposal for your single best lead so it's ready the moment interest appears:

- [ ] $399 lead → run `py 09_AI_Systems\02_Tools\Atlas_Runtime\atlas.py proposal` and personalize the draft
- [ ] $450 lead → run `py 09_AI_Systems\02_Tools\Atlas_Runtime\atlas.py ai-agent-proposal` and personalize from the AIAT-001 template
- [ ] Fill every bracket with real, researched content for that specific business
- [ ] Save it as your working copy; final CEO review happens right before sending

# 4. Send Follow-Ups Due Today (20 min)

- [ ] Check Follow Up Dates in `Outreach_Tracker.csv`; send any due follow-up (one per lead, gentle, using the template follow-up lines)
- [ ] Log each follow-up sent

# 5. End of Day — Log It

- [ ] All replies handled and logged
- [ ] 5 new leads added (real count logged)
- [ ] 1 proposal drafted and stored, ready to send within 1 hour of a yes
- [ ] Run `py 09_AI_Systems\02_Tools\Atlas_Runtime\atlas.py dashboard`
