# 5. Hard Boundaries

- No lead is ever invented. Every row in Lead_Tracker.csv is a real business you found.
- Nothing is sent, published, or posted without CEO (your own explicit) approval — and you press the button, not Atlas.
- The payment link never goes in a first cold message.
- No guarantees, no fake testimonials, no inflated claims — the no-hype rules (AOS_No_Hype_Revenue_Reality.md) apply all week.

---

# 6. Sprint Success Definition

Minimum: 25 real leads, 5 sent messages, 1 proposal delivered, 1 payment attempt (link sent or quote conversation started).
Target: first paid client.
Either way: lessons logged in Sprint_Lessons_Learned_Template.md — a documented failed week beats an undocumented lucky one.
