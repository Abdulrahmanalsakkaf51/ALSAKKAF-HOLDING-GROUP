# Column Guide

| Column | What to Record |
|--------|-----------------|
| Supplier ID | Sequential ID, e.g. SUP-001 |
| Supplier Name | Legal or trading name as given by the supplier |
| Product(s) Quoted | Which NESTLYRA product this quote applies to |
| MOQ | Minimum order quantity quoted, with unit |
| Unit Cost | Per-unit cost quoted, with currency |
| Sample Cost | Cost to obtain a physical sample, with currency, plus whether it is refundable |
| Lead Time | Quoted production and shipping lead time, stated as a range with source (e.g. "supplier states 20-30 days") |
| Communication Quality | Subjective rating (e.g. Poor / Fair / Good / Excellent) based on actual response speed and clarity, not assumed |
| Certifications | Any safety/compliance certification the supplier claims to hold — verify before recording as confirmed |
| Red Flags | Anything concerning: no verifiable business registration, refusal to provide samples, inconsistent answers, pressure tactics, unrealistic pricing |
| Notes | Anything else relevant to the decision |

---

# Rule

Do not add a row to this table for a supplier that has not actually been contacted. Do not fill in MOQ, cost, or lead time with an estimate presented as a quote — record only what the supplier has actually stated, and mark anything unconfirmed as `[VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH]`.
