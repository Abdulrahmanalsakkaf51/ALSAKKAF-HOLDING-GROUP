```text
[PAGE TITLE]
Our Standard

[INTRO]
Before anything carries the NESTLYRA name, it has to earn it. Here's
what we check, every time.

[STANDARD 1 — Calm by Design]
We choose products that reduce visual clutter, not add to it. If it
doesn't fit the calm, minimalist look we build around, it doesn't make
the collection — no matter how functional it is.

[STANDARD 2 — Built for Small Spaces]
We prioritize products designed for limited space: closets, shared
rooms, apartments. [VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH: specific fit/sizing claims
per product once dimensions are confirmed by the supplier]

[STANDARD 3 — Verified Before Listed]
Every product goes through our internal verification process before it
is listed as Active for sale. That process checks dimensions, materials,
weight, safety/compliance, supplier lead time, cost, minimum order
quantity, and a physical sample — see our Product Verification
Checklist. [VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH: link to a public-facing summary of
this process once one exists; the internal checklist itself
(`18_Product_Verification_Checklist.md`) is not customer-facing]

[STANDARD 4 — Honest Listings]
We do not publish a product page with invented specifications. If we
don't know a material, weight, or exact dimension yet, that product
stays in draft, not on the shelf.

[WHERE WE ARE TODAY]
NESTLYRA has not yet completed supplier selection or sample approval for
any product. [VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH: remove this line once at least one
product has passed the full verification process]
```
