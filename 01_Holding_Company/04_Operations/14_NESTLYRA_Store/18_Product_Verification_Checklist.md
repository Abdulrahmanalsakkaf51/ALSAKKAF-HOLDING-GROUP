# Purpose

Defines what must be confirmed before any NESTLYRA product moves from **DRAFT** to **Active** (visible and purchasable on the storefront). No product currently satisfies this checklist.

---

# Scope

Applies to every product in `15_Product_SpaceSaver_Vacuum_Storage_Set.md`, `16_Product_FlexFit_Drawer_Divider_Set.md`, and `17_Product_CalmFold_Wardrobe_Organizers.md`, and to any future product added to the catalog.

# Checklist — Complete Every Item Before DRAFT → Active

| # | Item | Confirmed? | Notes / Source |
|---|------|------------|-----------------|
| 1 | Dimensions confirmed by supplier spec sheet | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 2 | Material(s) confirmed by supplier spec sheet | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 3 | Weight confirmed | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 4 | Safety / compliance requirements identified for target market(s) | ☐ Not confirmed | e.g. flammability, chemical safety, labeling — jurisdiction depends on RAKEZ/legal entity outcome |
| 5 | Safety / compliance certification obtained where required | ☐ Not confirmed | Do not claim a certification the product does not hold |
| 6 | Supplier selected and comparison completed | ☐ Not confirmed | See `19_Supplier_Comparison_Sheet.md` |
| 7 | Supplier lead time confirmed | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 8 | Unit cost confirmed | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 9 | Minimum order quantity (MOQ) confirmed | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 10 | Landed cost calculated | ☐ Not confirmed | See `20_Landed_Cost_Calculator_Spec.md` |
| 11 | Target retail price set and margin checked against landed cost | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 12 | Physical sample ordered | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 13 | Physical sample inspected and approved | ☐ Not confirmed | See `21_Sample_Inspection_Checklist.md` |
| 14 | Real product photography taken | ☐ Not confirmed | No stock or concept images may substitute |
| 15 | Product page copy updated with real specs (no `[VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH]` markers remaining) | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 16 | Shipping and Returns policy pages updated to reflect this supplier's real terms | ☐ Not confirmed | — |
| 17 | Founder sign-off to move status from DRAFT to Active | ☐ Not confirmed | Founder decision, not an AI decision |

---

# Rule

A product may not move to Active status until **all 17 items** above are confirmed and checked by a human, with the source of each confirmation noted. Partial completion does not authorize a partial launch (e.g. do not publish a product page with real photos but a placeholder price).
