# Purpose

This folder holds the complete content and operations package for **NESTLYRA**, a private Shopify store (home organization / wardrobe products, calm-minimalist positioning) owned by ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP. It exists so that when the store is ready to move from draft to live, every page, policy, product template, and operational checklist has already been drafted, reviewed for honesty, and is waiting only on real-world confirmations — not on writing.

---

# Scope

This folder covers:

- All storefront copy (homepage, About, Our Standard, FAQ, Contact, Track Order, navigation/footer map).
- All required policy pages (Shipping, Returns, Privacy, Terms) in draft form with visible pending-status banners.
- Collection copy for the two planned collections.
- Product page templates for the three planned products, in DRAFT status.
- Operational checklists needed to move a product, a supplier, and the store itself from draft to live.

This folder does **not** cover:

- Any Shopify configuration, theme code, or app setup. No Shopify access has occurred and none is claimed anywhere in this folder.
- Any confirmed supplier, product spec, price, or delivery time. None exist yet.
- Any legal advice. Policy pages here are structural drafts only and require real legal review before publication.
