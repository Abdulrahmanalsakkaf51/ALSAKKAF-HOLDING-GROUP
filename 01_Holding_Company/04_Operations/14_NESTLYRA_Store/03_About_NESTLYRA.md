[PAGE TITLE]
About NESTLYRA

[BODY]
NESTLYRA is a home organization brand built around one idea: a well-kept
space should feel calm, not effortful.

We focus on wardrobe and small-space storage — the kind of everyday
organizing that quietly makes a room easier to live in. Our starting
point isn't more products. It's fewer, better-considered ones that fit
spaces that are already tight on room.

NESTLYRA is a new brand under ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP. We are building it
deliberately: choosing what we carry carefully, being honest about what
we don't know yet, and only opening the doors once we can stand behind
what's on the shelf.

[WHAT WE BELIEVE]
- Calm over clutter. A storage product should reduce visual noise, not
  add to it.
- Small spaces deserve real design thought, not scaled-down leftovers.
- Simplicity in what we sell, and in how we talk about it — no inflated
  claims, no manufactured urgency.

[WHERE WE ARE TODAY]
NESTLYRA is currently in setup, not open for orders. We are working
through supplier selection and business registration before anything
goes live. [VERIFY BEFORE LAUNCH: remove or update this paragraph once
the store is genuinely open for orders]

- No founding date, years-in-business, or "trusted by X customers" language is used, because none of that is true yet for a brand that has not launched.
- No supplier, factory, or sourcing-region claims are made. None have been selected. See `18_Product_Verification_Checklist.md` and `19_Supplier_Comparison_Sheet.md`.
- The "Where We Are Today" paragraph is intentionally honest about pre-launch status and should stay on the live page until the store is genuinely accepting orders, at which point it should be revised or removed by a human, not silently deleted.
