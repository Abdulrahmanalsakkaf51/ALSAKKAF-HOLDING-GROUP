# Purpose

Defines what must be physically checked when a supplier sample arrives, before that sample — and therefore that supplier and product — can be approved.

---

# Scope

Applies to any physical sample received for SpaceSaver Vacuum Storage Set, FlexFit Drawer Divider Set, CalmFold Wardrobe Organizers, or any future NESTLYRA product. No sample has been received to date.

---

# Checklist — Complete on Arrival of Every Sample

| # | Check | Result | Notes |
|---|-------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Material matches what the supplier quoted | ☐ Not yet checked | Compare against supplier spec sheet |
| 2 | Stitching / build quality is acceptable | ☐ Not yet checked | Check seams, joints, fasteners, or moving parts as applicable |
| 3 | Dimensions match the quoted spec | ☐ Not yet checked | Measure directly, do not estimate |
| 4 | Weight matches the quoted spec | ☐ Not yet checked | Weigh directly |
| 5 | Packaging condition on arrival | ☐ Not yet checked | Note any damage from transit separately from manufacturing defects |
| 6 | Visible defects present | ☐ Not yet checked | List each defect found, however minor |
| 7 | Functional test performed (if applicable) | ☐ Not yet checked | e.g. does a divider adjust properly, does a vacuum bag seal properly |
| 8 | Odor / off-gassing check | ☐ Not yet checked | Note if present; do not dismiss as normal without basis |
| 9 | Photos taken of the sample (all sides, packaging, any defect) | ☐ Not yet checked | Photos are required documentation, not optional |
| 10 | Comparison against Product Verification Checklist fields | ☐ Not yet checked | Cross-check against `18_Product_Verification_Checklist.md` |
| 11 | Overall approval decision | ☐ Not yet decided | Approve / Reject / Request Revised Sample — Founder decision |

---

# Rule

A sample is not approved by default. It is approved only when a human has completed every check above and explicitly recorded an "Approve" decision with dated notes. A missed check is treated as "not approved," not as a pass.
