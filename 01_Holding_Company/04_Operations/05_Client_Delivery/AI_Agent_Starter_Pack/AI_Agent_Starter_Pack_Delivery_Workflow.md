# 4. Quality Gates

1. No package leaves without every deliverable in Section 1 present.
2. Every prompt must have been tested on at least 2 realistic sample tasks before delivery.
3. The safety rules document is mandatory — never deliver agents without approval boundaries.
4. The Founder personally reviews and approves the package before handover.
5. Honest language only: agents are AI assistants under the client's approval, never "AI employees."

---

# 5. What This Service Never Includes

Fully autonomous AI employees, guaranteed revenue, trading profits, automatic email sending, automatic account creation, paid integrations, API automation, or custom software development — unless separately quoted and approved in writing.
