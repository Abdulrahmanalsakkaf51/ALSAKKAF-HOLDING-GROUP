# 1. How to Use

Send this form to the client after the engagement is confirmed. The 5-7 business day delivery clock starts when complete answers are received. Short answers are fine — honest answers matter more than long ones.

---

# 2. About Your Business

1. What does your business do, in one or two sentences?
2. Who are your customers?
3. How many people work in the business (including you)?
4. What are the 3 biggest time-wasters in your week right now?
5. Which AI tools (if any) do you already use, and how?

# 3. For Your Manager Agent

6. What does a typical working day look like for you?
7. What decisions do you make repeatedly?
8. What slips through the cracks most often (follow-ups, tasks, deadlines)?
9. How do you currently track your priorities (app, notebook, memory)?
10. What would a perfect end-of-day summary tell you?

# 4. For Your Research / Analyst Agent

11. Who are your 3 main competitors (names or types)?
12. What do you wish you knew about your customers?
13. What topics do you need to stay informed about?
14. Do you create content (posts, videos, newsletters)? About what?
15. What is one business question you have never had time to research properly?

# 5. For Your Operations / Implementation Agent

16. What is one process in your business that only exists in your head?
17. What tasks do you explain to people over and over?
18. What reports do you produce (or wish you produced) regularly?
19. When you decide to do something new, what usually blocks it from happening?

# 6. Boundaries and Preferences

20. What should the agents never do without your approval?
21. What language(s) should the agents work in?
22. Is there anything confidential we should know NOT to include in the agent designs?
23. Preferred time for your 30-minute setup session (2-3 options)?

---

# 7. What Happens Next

Once your answers arrive, we build your three agents over 5-7 business days, review everything internally, and deliver the full package with your setup session invitation. Nothing is connected to your accounts and no software is installed — you receive documented agents you run inside your own AI tool.
