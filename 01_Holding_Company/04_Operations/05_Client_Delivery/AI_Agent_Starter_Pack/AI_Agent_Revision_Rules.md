# 1. What's Included

One revision round within 7 days of handover, covering:

- Tone, wording, or language adjustments to any agent prompt
- Adding or refining responsibilities within an agent's existing role
- Fixes to the routing map, workflow, or dashboard template
- Correcting anything that doesn't match your intake answers

A revision round is one consolidated list of requested changes, delivered back within 3 business days.

---

# 2. What's Not Included (quoted separately if wanted)

- A fourth agent or a new agent type
- Changing an agent's fundamental role (e.g., turning the Research Agent into a sales agent)
- Integrations, API connections, automation software, or custom code
- Ongoing monthly support or agent maintenance
- Work based on new business directions not present in the original intake

---

# 3. How to Request a Revision

1. Collect all requested changes into one email to atlasos5555@gmail.com (temporary launch contact email).
2. For each item, say which document it concerns and what outcome you want.
3. We confirm scope within 1 business day, then deliver within 3 business days.

---

# 4. After the Window

Small questions are always welcome. Larger changes after the 7-day window are scoped and quoted as a new engagement — often a small one.
