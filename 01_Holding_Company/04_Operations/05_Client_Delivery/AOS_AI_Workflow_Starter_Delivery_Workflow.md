# 1A. The Offer: 3 Automations for the Price of One

This single, scoped engagement delivers three concrete outcomes for the client, not three times the work:

| Automation | What It Covers | Maps to Stages |
|------------|------------------|-----------------|
| 1 — Lead / Client Tracking Automation | A working system to capture, track, and follow up on leads/clients | Stage 3 (Workflow Review) + Stage 6 (Dashboard Plan, tracking view) |
| 2 — Internal Operations Automation | One repetitive internal process documented, mapped, and made repeatable | Stage 4 (AI Workflow Map) + Stage 5 (Document Structure) + Stage 7 (Implementation Checklist) |
| 3 — CEO / Management Visibility Automation | A lightweight view of what's happening in the business | Stage 6 (Dashboard Plan, visibility view) + Stage 8 (Follow-Up Recommendation) |

All three automations are produced within the same 5-7 business day timeline and the same $399 USD price — the same six underlying deliverables (Stages 3-8 below), reframed around three business outcomes the client can immediately recognize.

# 2. Workflow Stages

| Stage | What Happens | Owner | Template |
|-------|--------------|-------|----------|
| 1. Intake | Client completes intake form | Client + Founder | `Client_Intake_Form_Template.md` (RLCD-002) |
| 2. Discovery | Founder/Atlas reviews intake, asks clarifying questions if needed | Atlas drafts questions, Founder sends | `Discovery_Questions_Template.md` (RLOUT-008) |

| 5. Document Structure Recommendation | Recommend folder/file organization | Atlas drafts, Founder reviews | `Document_Structure_Recommendation_Template.md` (RLCD-005) |
| 6. Dashboard Plan | Recommend a simple reporting approach | Atlas drafts, Founder reviews | `Dashboard_Plan_Template.md` (RLCD-006) |
| 7. Implementation Checklist | Steps to put the workflow into daily use | Atlas drafts, Founder reviews | `Implementation_Checklist_Template.md` (RLCD-007) |
| 8. Follow-Up Recommendation | One concrete next step once running | Atlas drafts, Founder reviews | Included in Final Client Report |
| 9. Final Report | Bundle all deliverables into one client-facing document | Atlas assembles, Founder approves | `Final_Client_Report_Template.md` (RLCD-008) |

# 4. Who Does What

- **Atlas** drafts every deliverable from client-provided intake information. Atlas never invents client details it wasn't given.
- **Founder** reviews and approves every deliverable before it reaches the client, confirms delivery, and handles all client communication.
- **Guardian** reviews if the client's intake data includes anything sensitive (e.g. if the client describes internal financial or credential-handling processes) — Guardian never receives real credentials.
- **The Librarian** indexes the finished, anonymized deliverable templates/patterns (not raw client data) for reuse in future engagements.
