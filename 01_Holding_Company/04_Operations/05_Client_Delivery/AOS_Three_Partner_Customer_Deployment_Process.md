# 1. What This Process Covers

The AOS AI Agent Starter Pack: $450 USD, one scoped engagement, 3 custom Partner (agent) roles, delivered in 5-7 business days from complete intake answers. Payment link: the approved PayPal checkout on alsakkafsystems.com.

---

# 2. Deployment Stages

| # | Stage | What happens | Gate |
|---|-------|--------------|------|
| 1 | Payment | Client pays via the approved PayPal link | PayPal confirmation |
| 2 | Client intake | Client answers the intake questionnaire (business, workflow, goals, constraints) | Complete answers received |
| 3 | Workflow discovery | We map the actual workflow the Partners will serve | Client confirms the map |
| 4 | Data classification | Identify what data the Partners may read; anything private/regulated is excluded or gated | Client + Founder sign-off |
| 5 | Partner selection | Choose the 3 roles (e.g., Manager, Research/Analyst, Operations) fitted to the workflow | Client confirms roles |
| 6 | Supervisor / router configuration | Define task routing between the roles and what escalates to humans | Founder review |
| 7 | AI runtime selection | Choose a deployment option (Section 4) | Client decision |
| 8 | Knowledge connection | Connect approved documents/templates the Partners reference | Data classification respected |
| 9 | Permissions | Set authority levels and approval rules (drafts only; humans send/decide) | Founder review |
| 10 | Testing | Run role test tasks against acceptance criteria | Test log |
| 11 | Human acceptance | Client reviews outputs and accepts or requests the included revision | Client acceptance |
| 12 | Deployment | Deliver profiles, prompts, routing map, workflow, dashboard template, guides | Delivery checklist |
| 13 | Handover | 30-minute explanation/setup session; safety and approval rules walked through | Session held |
| 14 | Support | Post-delivery questions per the pack's support terms; larger changes quoted separately | — |

---

# 3. Scope Honesty — the $450 Starter Includes / Does Not Include

Includes: 3 custom Partner roles (profiles + prompts), task routing map, business-specific workflow, dashboard/reporting template, implementation guide, approval/safety rules, one revision, a 30-minute session.

Does NOT automatically include: enterprise integrations, unlimited AI usage, private infrastructure, nine-Partner department pods, enterprise dashboards, regulated-data deployment, custom software development, or AI subscription costs. Each of those is scoped and quoted separately.

---

# 5. Standing Safety Rules

1. Partners draft; humans approve and send.
2. No credentials are ever collected or stored by us.
3. No regulated or private-sensitive data enters a deployment without explicit classification and a custom scope.
4. No capability claims beyond what testing demonstrated.
