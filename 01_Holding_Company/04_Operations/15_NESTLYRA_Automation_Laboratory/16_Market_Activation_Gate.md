# Purpose

Define the single gate every country must pass before it can become a live NESTLYRA Market, so international expansion happens country by country on evidence, never as a bulk "sell everywhere" switch.

---

# Scope

Covers: the activation criteria, the initial-Markets cap, and the verified current Market state. Does not cover: the per-country evidence records — those live in the Market Readiness Register (NAL-017) — or the activation click itself, which is a Founder action inside Shopify.

# 2. Gate Criteria — Every Country, Every Time

A country passes this gate only when ALL ten criteria hold, each backed by evidence recorded in the Market Readiness Register (NAL-017).

| # | Criterion | Evidence Standard |
|---|-----------|-------------------|
| 1 | Supplier ships to the country | Supplier evidence on record (NAL-010) — supplier confirmation, not marketplace listing text |
| 2 | Tracking exists for the route | Tracked-delivery evidence for this destination (NAL-010) |
| 3 | Realistic delivery range verified | Evidence-based range, not the store's provisional display estimates (U-15) |
| 4 | Shipping cost known | Actual route cost on record — current AED rates are PROVISIONAL / UNVERIFIED (U-03) |
| 5 | Duties / import-tax treatment documented | Who pays, at what threshold, collected how — recorded, not guessed (U-04) |
| 6 | Product restrictions checked | Destination-country restrictions for the product category verified (NAL-013 restrictions section) |
| 7 | Return route and cost known | A workable, costed return path exists (NAL-020) — "no returns possible" is a FAIL |
| 8 | Customer support can serve the Market | Language, time zone, and channel coverage confirmed against NAL-019 capacity |
| 9 | Unit economics acceptable | Per-Market landed-cost calculation (NAL-011) shows acceptable margin including return cost |
| 10 | Founder approves | Founder decision recorded in the Founder Approval Queue (NAL-014) |

---

# 3. Gate Rules

1. Initial live Markets are capped at a maximum of 1-3 verified countries. "Activate every country" is forbidden at launch and after it.
2. No specific first Market is recommended in this document or anywhere else today — no supplier evidence exists (U-09), so any recommendation would be invented. Recommendations become possible only when NAL-017 rows carry real evidence.
3. A criterion with UNKNOWN evidence is a FAIL for gating purposes. UNKNOWN never passes.
4. Passing the gate authorizes nothing by itself. The Founder personally performs any Market activation in Shopify; the Partner may never activate a Market (NAL-006, forbidden action 2).
5. Countries that fail go to the Deferred Market Register (NAL-017 section 3) with a reason and a revisit trigger — deferral is a valid, recorded outcome.
6. UAE remains the only active Market until this gate produces a Founder-approved change.
