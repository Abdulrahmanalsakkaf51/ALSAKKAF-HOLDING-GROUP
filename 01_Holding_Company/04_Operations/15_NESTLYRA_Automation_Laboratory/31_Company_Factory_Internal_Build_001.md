# Purpose

Register PRJ-020 as The Company Factory — Internal Build 001: the first internal application of the "company factory" pattern, in which AOS builds a controlled, evidence-gated operating system for one of its own ventures (a commerce company) before ever offering that capability outward.

# Scope

- Registers as the system of record (NAL-008..NAL-027), reports reconciling to registers only (NAL-026/029).
