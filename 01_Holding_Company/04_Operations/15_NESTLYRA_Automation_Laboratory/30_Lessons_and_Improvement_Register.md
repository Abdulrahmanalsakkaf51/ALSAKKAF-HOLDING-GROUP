# 2. Register Rules

1. A lesson row is factual — what actually happened, with its source.
2. An improvement is a proposal until the Founder (or the standard the Founder already approved) covers it; the Approval Status column records which.
3. Lessons that generalize beyond NESTLYRA are candidates for the Company Factory record (NAL-031) — marked there as reusable only after Founder review.
4. Nothing in this register modifies a workflow by itself — a lesson's improvement lands only through an approved document revision.
