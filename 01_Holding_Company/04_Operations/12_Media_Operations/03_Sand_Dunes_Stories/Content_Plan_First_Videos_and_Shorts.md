# 4. Production Notes

1. Videos 1, 2, and 3 launch the channel — strongest hooks, clearest sourcing.
2. Every script gets a source list in the archive; claims with thin evidence are framed as legend or debate on screen (MEDIA-004 sourcing rule).
3. Episode 9 (folklore) must carry an on-screen "folklore" label in the first 30 seconds — this protects the channel's credibility.
4. Visual licensing is the main production cost — the licensing decision per episode is recorded in the archive before drafting begins.
