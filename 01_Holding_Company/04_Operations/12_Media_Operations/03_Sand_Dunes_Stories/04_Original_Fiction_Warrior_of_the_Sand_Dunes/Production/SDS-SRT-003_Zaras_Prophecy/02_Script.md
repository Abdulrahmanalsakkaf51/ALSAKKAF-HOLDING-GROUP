# 3. Storyboard Beats

1. **Title card.** A modest desert campfire at night, isolated from any city.
2. **Zara at work.** Zara reading something (sand, palm, fire — left undefined pending Founder's preferred seer-mechanic), travelers implied around her over time.
3. **Malik arrives.** A boy in a plain cloak sitting across the fire, deliberately unremarkable framing — he should not visually "read" as special.
4. **Zara's stillness.** Close on Zara's face, a shift from routine to recognition.
5. **The declaration.** Zara's head lowering — a gesture of respect, not fear.
6. **Malik's disbelief.** A brief, light reaction — this should read as genuine disbelief/humor, not mockery of Zara.
7. **Zara's correction.** Zara looking directly at Malik — firm, certain, final beat before the end card.
