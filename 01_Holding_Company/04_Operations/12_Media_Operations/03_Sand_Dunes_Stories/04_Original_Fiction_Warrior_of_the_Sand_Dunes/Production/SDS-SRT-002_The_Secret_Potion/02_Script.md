# 3. Storyboard Beats

1. **Title card.** Quiet desert campsite at dusk.
2. **Establishing.** Leila and Malik sitting apart from Amir, a private moment framed to feel intimate rather than public.
3. **The vial.** Close composition on the clay vial passing from Leila's hands to Malik's.
4. **Malik's question.** Reaction shot on Malik's face — confusion, not fear.
5. **Leila's warning.** Close on Leila's face, serious, direct eye contact implied.
6. **Malik processing.** Malik looking down at the vial, unreadable expression.
7. **The hold.** Malik's hand closing around the vial — final visual beat before the end card.
8. **End card.** Series branding, "continues" text.
