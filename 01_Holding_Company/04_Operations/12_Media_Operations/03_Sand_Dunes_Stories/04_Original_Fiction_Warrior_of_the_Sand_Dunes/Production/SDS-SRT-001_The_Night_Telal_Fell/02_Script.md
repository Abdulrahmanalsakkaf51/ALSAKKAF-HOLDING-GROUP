# 3. Storyboard Beats (visual description per card)

1. **Title card.** Telal's skyline at golden hour, warm and peaceful, before any sign of the coup — title text overlaid.
2. **Feroos's betrayal.** A commander's silhouette kneeling before a throne, then rising — composition should read as a status/power reversal in one image (framing, not violence).
3. **The guards.** Palace gate torches, guards standing but visually "changed" (posture, allegiance implied through color/lighting shift, not action).
4. **Leila and the boys.** Three silhouettes in motion through a corridor or gate, urgency conveyed through motion blur / dynamic framing.
5. **The burning towers.** Telal's skyline again, now lit orange/red — direct visual contrast to the title card's golden calm.
6. **Into the desert.** Wide shot, three small figures against a huge dune landscape — scale emphasizes vulnerability.
7. **End card.** Series title lockup + "Episode 1 coming soon" + channel branding.
