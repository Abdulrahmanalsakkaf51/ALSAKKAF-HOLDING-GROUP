# 2. Honesty Note on the Title

The word "كذبوا" is a rhetorical hook. The script's opening explicitly reframes it: these scientists did not lie in the sense of deception — they were certain, and their certainty was wrong, and they used their authority to silence correct ideas. This framing is stated on screen in the first 30 seconds so the title's promise stays honest.
