# 1. Purpose and Rights Boundary

The Founder supplied a reference video ("لما العلماء كذبوا… والطبيعة صارت أذكى منهم!") as **style and pacing inspiration only**. This treatment uses only the observed structural characteristics listed below — it does not copy, trace, or reproduce that video's exact artwork, voice, music, frames, channel branding, or any other copyrighted asset. Nothing from the reference is an asset source; it is a pacing benchmark.

Reference characteristics identified (structure only, not content):

- Approximately 4 minutes
- Vertical format
- Frequent visual changes, roughly every 2-4 seconds
- Illustrated documentary approach
- Maps, diagrams, source screenshots, and a recurring presenter
- Strong Arabic on-screen explanations
- Active, continuous narration
- Visual movement rather than static slides

---

# 2. How رحلة عقل's Version Will Be Original

رحلة عقل already has an established identity (MEDIA-004): "cinematic, warm, measured narration — history told as story, folklore told as folklore." The reference's pacing discipline (frequent visual change, no static slides) is worth adopting; its energetic explainer tone is not automatically a fit for a channel whose differentiator is measured, honestly-sourced storytelling. This treatment reconciles the two:

| Reference trait | رحلة عقل's original take |
|------------------|----------------------------|
| Recurring presenter/avatar | An original, Founder-designed narrator avatar or motif specific to رحلة عقل — not a generic explainer-channel host archetype. Design not yet commissioned; this is a requirement flag, not a finished asset. |
| Maps/diagrams/source screenshots | Used, but drawn from رحلة عقل's own sourcing discipline (MEDIA-004 Section 5) — every map or diagram traces to a cited source in the episode's research record, consistent with the channel's honesty differentiator |
| Frequent visual change (2-4s) | Adopted as a pacing target for information-dense segments, but folklore-labeled segments (MEDIA-004 pillar 4) may hold slightly longer on a single strong image when the moment calls for gravity rather than speed — pacing serves the story, not a fixed rule |
| Active, continuous narration | Adopted, replacing the rejected generic Piper voice with a real voice per MEDIA-017 Section 7 (candidate research in progress) |
| Illustrated documentary approach | Adopted as the visual language, using original illustration/diagram work — never real photographs of real places presented without sourcing, and never another creator's illustration style |

---

# 3. What This Treatment Does Not Authorize

- Does not authorize copying the reference video's specific compositions, character/avatar design, music cues, or on-screen graphic templates
- Does not authorize claiming the reference's channel branding, name, or handle in any form
- Does not authorize production of a full video under this treatment alone — per MEDIA-017 Section 8, a 20-30 second proof segment must be produced and pass creative + technical QC first
