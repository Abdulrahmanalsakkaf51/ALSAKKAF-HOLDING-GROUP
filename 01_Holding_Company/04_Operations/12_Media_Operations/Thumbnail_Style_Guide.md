# 1. Purpose

This guide keeps thumbnails consistent, honest, and fast to produce across the three channels. It is written so a Partner can generate a complete thumbnail brief (per CFOS-001) without new design decisions each time.

---

# 2. Rules for All Channels

1. The thumbnail must show something that actually appears in the video. No bait imagery.
2. Maximum 4 words of thumbnail text. Text must be readable at 120 pixels wide.
3. One focal subject per thumbnail. No collages.
4. Use each channel's fixed palette and font (Section 3). Do not invent new styles per video.
5. No red arrows, fake shock faces, or artificial red circles.
6. No client names, private data, or unreleased material.
7. Source images must be owned, licensed, or original renders — same rights rule as footage.
8. Export at 1280x720 (16:9), under 2 MB, JPG or PNG.

---

# 3. Channel Styles

| Element | ALSAKKAF Systems | رحلة عقل | Sand Dunes Stories |
|---------|------------------|----------|--------------------|
| Mood | Clean, technical, trustworthy | Calm, thoughtful, minimal | Cinematic, warm, mysterious |
| Background | Dark navy or off-white with the geometric-A brand mark | Single soft color field or paper texture | Desert photography or illustration, golden-hour tones |
| Text language | English | Arabic (large, right-aligned) | English (serif or classic sans) |
| Text color | White or brand gold on dark; navy on light | High-contrast single color | Warm white or sand gold |
| Recurring element | Brand geometric-A from Brand_Assets | Small mind/journey icon used consistently | Silhouette or artifact from the story |
| Face usage | Optional, natural expression only | Optional | Rarely — story imagery leads |

Brand marks and colors come from `09_AI_Systems/02_Tools/Brand_Assets` and the PRJ-010 brand identity documents; do not redraw the logo.

---

# 4. Production Steps

1. The thumbnail brief (from CFOS-001 Section 6) states: focal subject, text (max 4 words), palette, and the exact claim the image makes.
2. The image is produced from owned or licensed sources.
3. The Content Review Checklist (MEDIA-010) thumbnail items are checked.
4. Founder approves the thumbnail together with the video before publishing.
