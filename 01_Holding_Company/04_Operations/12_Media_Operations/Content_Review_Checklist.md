# 1. Purpose

This checklist runs at Step 8 of the Video Workflow Operating Process (MEDIA-009), before any package reaches Founder approval. It applies to long-form videos and Shorts on all three channels.

---

# 2. Rights and Safety

| # | Check | Pass condition |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 1 | Footage rights | All footage owned, licensed, or explicitly permitted; license noted in the archive |
| 2 | Music rights | Music owned/licensed; license noted |
| 3 | Images and fonts | Thumbnail and graphics sources are owned or licensed |
| 4 | Private data | No client names, student data, credentials, or internal-private files visible in any frame or screen recording |
| 5 | People and consent | Any recognizable person has given consent; no AI-generated likeness of a real person |

---

# 3. Honesty and Accuracy

| # | Check | Pass condition |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 6 | Title accuracy | Title describes what the video actually shows |
| 7 | Thumbnail promise | Thumbnail subject appears in the video |
| 8 | Capability claims | Any AOS/AI claim stays within what testing demonstrated (CDP-001 rule) |
| 9 | Facts and stories | Historical/factual claims checked; folklore clearly framed as folklore (Sand Dunes Stories) |
| 10 | Numbers | Any statistic on screen has a source; no invented figures |

---

# 4. Quality and Brand

| # | Check | Pass condition |
|---|-------|----------------|
| 11 | Audio | Voice clear, levels consistent, no clipping |
| 12 | Language | Script language reviewed (Arabic for رحلة عقل; English for the others); no filler-heavy passages |
| 13 | Thumbnail style | Matches Thumbnail_Style_Guide.md for the channel; max 4 words |
| 14 | Description | First two lines carry the hook; correct links only (approved links, no tracking gimmicks) |
| 15 | End screen / CTA | One clear, honest call to action |

# 5. Administration
