# 1. Purpose

This plan operationalizes `AOS_Multi_Platform_Content_Factory.md` (STRAT-010) into a concrete, repeatable weekly workflow for the Revenue Launch. Its single job is to turn content into qualified conversations about the AOS AI Workflow Starter Pack ($399 USD) — not to chase viral reach for its own sake.

Nothing in this document authorizes publishing. Every piece of content drafted under this plan requires explicit CEO approval before it goes live, per PRJ-007's CEO Approval Gates.

2. Business systems
3. Productivity
4. Dashboards
5. Workflow automation

Every piece of content should map to at least one pillar. No content pillar is platform-exclusive — the same idea can be adapted across platforms in different formats.

---

# 4. Weekly Batching Workflow

Content is **batched weekly**, not filmed/written live every day:

1. **Weekly planning (30 min):** pick 5-7 topics from the pillars above, log them in `Content_Calendar.csv` with Status "Idea."
2. **Batch writing/recording (1 session):** scripts and captions are drafted from `Short_Form_Video_Script_Bank.md` and `AOS_Service_Content_Posts.md`; Status moves to "Scripted" then "Recorded" as work happens.
3. **Batch editing (1 session):** edited content moves to "Edited," then "Queued."
4. **CEO review:** CEO reviews queued content; approved items move to "Approved."
5. **Daily publishing:** one approved item goes out per day per active platform, moving Status to "Posted" only after the CEO-approved publish action actually happens.

This keeps daily execution to "press publish on something already approved," not "create something new every day."

# 6. Content Standards

- **Original-content rule:** content must be owned, licensed, public domain, or meaningfully transformed with original commentary/education — no stolen clips, no low-effort reposts.
- **No fake results.** Never show or claim a result, testimonial, or number that hasn't actually happened.
- **No income guarantees.** Never imply a client (or the Founder) is guaranteed a specific revenue outcome from using AI or from this offer.
- **No misleading AI claims.** Never imply AI is magic, fully autonomous, or a replacement for the business owner's judgment.
- **Honest offer framing.** When the $399 AI Workflow Starter Pack is mentioned, framing must be accurate: what it includes, who it's for, and that other offers are "Request Custom Quote."
