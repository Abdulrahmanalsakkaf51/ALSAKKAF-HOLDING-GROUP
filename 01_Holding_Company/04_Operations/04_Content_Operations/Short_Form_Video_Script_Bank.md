# Purpose

Ten original short-form video scripts (Shorts / Reels / TikTok, ~30-60 seconds each) for the Revenue Launch content system. Every script is a draft only — none are recorded or published until the CEO approves, per `Multi_Platform_Content_Execution_Plan.md` (RLCONTENT-001). No fake results, no guaranteed outcomes, no misleading AI claims.

---

# Script 1 — "3 Signs Your Business Needs an AI Workflow"

| Field | Content |
|-------|---------|
| Pillar | AI for small business |
| Hook | "If you're still copy-pasting the same email every week, this is for you." |
| Body | Name three concrete signs: repeating the same document/task weekly, no clear place client info lives, spending evenings on admin instead of clients. Explain briefly what an "AI workflow" means in plain terms — a documented, repeatable process an AI tool helps you run faster, not magic automation. |
| CTA | "Follow for more practical AI tips for small business." |
| Platform notes | Works on TikTok, Reels, Shorts as-is; add on-screen text for the 3 signs |
