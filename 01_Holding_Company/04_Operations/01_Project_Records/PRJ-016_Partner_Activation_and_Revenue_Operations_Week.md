# 1. Objective

Turn the built Partner packages and deterministic tools into a controlled internal revenue operation:

1. Verify Partner readiness with prompt-level and practical role tests.
2. Guardian activation risk review per Partner.
3. Prepare Founder activation packages (decision summaries + ADR drafts). No Partner is marked Active without Founder approval.
4. Build a real, verified prospect queue (10 prospects, official sources, re-verifiable).
5. Produce 5 personalized outreach drafts — NOT SENT, Founder-approved sending only.
6. Give Atlas a runnable, honest daily revenue briefing.
