# 1. What We Are Launching First

**AOS AI Services.** Not YouTube, not dropshipping, not a software product — services first, because a single client can be found, sold, and paid within days using skills AOS already has documented (`AOS_Service_Offer_Catalog.md`, STRAT-007). Content (STRAT-010) and a lightweight website (STRAT-012) run in support of lead generation from day one. Dropshipping (STRAT-011) stays a small, controlled experiment, not a business line, until services revenue is proven.

This sequencing — one engine first, not five at once — is the core CTO recommendation behind this entire package (`AOS_Revenue_Launch_Master_Plan.md`, STRAT-005).
