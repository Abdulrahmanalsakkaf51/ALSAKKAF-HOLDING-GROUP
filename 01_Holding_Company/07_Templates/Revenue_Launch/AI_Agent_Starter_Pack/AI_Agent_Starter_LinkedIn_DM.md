# 1. Rules

1. Connect first with a short note; pitch only after they accept, or use one combined message where the platform allows.
2. Personalized, CEO-approved, manually sent. No automation tools, ever.
3. No payment link. No pricing in the first message unless asked.

---

# 2. Connection Note (300-character limit)

> Hi [first name] — I build custom AI agent setups for [business type]s. Saw [specific thing about their business/post]. Would value connecting.

# 3. First DM After Accept

Hi [first name], thanks for connecting.

Quick context on what I do: I set up three custom AI assistants for small business owners — one that manages your daily priorities, one that does your research (competitors, customers, content), and one that turns decisions into checklists and workflows. Built around your business specifically, delivered in about a week, with a live setup session.

[One line connecting it to something real about their business.]

Open to a short call to see if it's useful for [business name]? If a call's too much, I can send a one-page overview here instead.

# 4. Follow-Up (once, 4-5 days later)

Hi [first name] — no pressure on this. If it's not the season for it, all good. Want me to just send the one-pager so you have it?
