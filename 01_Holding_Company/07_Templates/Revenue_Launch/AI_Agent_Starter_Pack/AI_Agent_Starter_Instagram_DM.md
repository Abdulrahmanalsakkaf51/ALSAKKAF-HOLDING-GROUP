# 1. Rules

1. Engage genuinely first (a real comment or reply to a story) before DMing, when possible.
2. Personalized, CEO-approved, manually sent. Never bulk, never bots.
3. Keep it short — Instagram DMs are conversations, not letters.
4. No payment link, no hard pitch in the first message.

---

# 2. Opening DM

> Hey [first name]! [Genuine line about their business or recent post — specific, not flattery.] Quick question — are you using AI for the day-to-day side of the business (planning, research, content ideas), or is it all still manual?

# 3. If They Reply With Interest

> That's exactly what I do — I build three custom AI assistants for business owners: one manages your priorities and daily plan, one handles research (competitors, customers, content ideas), and one turns your decisions into checklists and workflows. All built around [business name] specifically, ready in about a week, with a setup call included. Want me to send a short overview?

# 4. If They Ask the Price

> $450 one-time — that covers all three agents, the setup docs, and a 30-min session with me. No subscription. We'd confirm the details by email before anything is paid.

# 5. Closing / No Reply

One gentle follow-up after 4-5 days maximum:

> No worries if this isn't the moment — I'll leave the door open. If you ever want the one-page overview, just say the word.
