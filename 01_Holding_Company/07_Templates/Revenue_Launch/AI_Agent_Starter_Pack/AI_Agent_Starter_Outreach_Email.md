# 1. Rules

1. Every message is personalized to a real, researched lead — never mass-sent.
2. CEO approves each message before it is sent. Sending is always manual.
3. No payment link in first contact. No price in the subject line.
4. No fake claims ("I noticed you struggle with...") unless the observation is real and specific.

**Subject options (pick one, personalize):**

Hi [first name],
