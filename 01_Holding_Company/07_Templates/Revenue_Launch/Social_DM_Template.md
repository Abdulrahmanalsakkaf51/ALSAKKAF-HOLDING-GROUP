# Purpose

Draft short social DMs (LinkedIn, Instagram) for warm or referred leads. **Draft only. Requires CEO approval of content and recipient before sending.**

---

# Template A — LinkedIn, warm connection

Hi [First Name] — saw [Company Name]'s post about [specific detail]. I help small businesses set up simple AI workflows and dashboards without a big tech project. Worth a quick chat sometime?

---

# Template B — Instagram, referral

Hey [First Name]! [Referrer Name] mentioned you might be looking to clean up [specific process, e.g. "your content scheduling"]. I do small, focused AI/ops projects — happy to share a couple of examples if that's useful.

---

# Template C — LinkedIn, cold but specific

Hi [First Name] — noticed [Company Name] is [specific observation, e.g. "growing its online orders"]. I build lightweight dashboards and AI workflows for teams at that stage. Open to a short intro call?

---

# How to Use

1. Personalize with one real, specific detail — no generic mass-copy text.
2. Submit the filled draft and recipient to the CEO for approval before sending.
3. Log the approved/sent status in RLT-001 (Lead Tracker), not in this file.
