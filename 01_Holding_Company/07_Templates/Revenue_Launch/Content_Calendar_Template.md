# Purpose

---

# Template

| Date | Platform | Content Type | Topic / Pillar | Status | Owner |
|------|----------|---------------|------------------|--------|-------|
| [YYYY-MM-DD] | [YouTube/Instagram/TikTok/Facebook/Threads/LinkedIn] | [Long-form/Short/Reel/Post] | [Pillar name] | Idea | [Owner] |
| 2026-07-14 | Instagram | Reel | AI for small business | Scripted | Atlas draft |
| 2026-07-15 | LinkedIn | Post | Behind the build | Idea | CEO |

Status values: Idea, Scripted, Recorded, Edited, Queued, Approved, Posted.

---

# How to Use

1. Move each row through the Status column in order.
2. "Approved" means the CEO has reviewed and approved the content for posting.
3. "Posted" only happens after CEO approval — this template does not publish anything itself. See STRAT-010.
