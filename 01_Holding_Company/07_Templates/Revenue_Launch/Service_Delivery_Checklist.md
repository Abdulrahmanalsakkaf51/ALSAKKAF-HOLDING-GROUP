# How to Use

1. Check each step off in order; do not skip requirements confirmation.
2. "Invoice sent" and "Payment received" require a CEO-approved invoicing/payment method — this template does not define one itself.
3. Once complete, use RLT-007 (Client Report Template) for the client-facing summary.
