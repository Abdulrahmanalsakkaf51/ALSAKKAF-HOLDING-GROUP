# How to Use

1. Fill in [Company Name] / [Your Name] / [Location] / [link] consistently across all platforms.
2. Do not create the real account from this template — the CEO creates or approves account creation manually per STRAT-009.
3. Store the approved bio text here; never store account passwords, recovery codes, or 2FA secrets in this file.
