# Purpose

Concrete discovery questions to ask before recommending the AI Workflow Starter Pack or a custom quote.

---

# Questions

```text
1. Walk me through how [the process they mentioned] works today, step by step.
2. Roughly how much time does your team spend on this per week?
3. What tools are you currently using for it (spreadsheets, email, paper, specific software)?
4. Who else on your team is involved in this process?
5. What have you already tried to fix or improve this?
6. How urgent is solving this for you right now — is there a deadline or trigger event?
7. If this were solved well, what would that free you up to focus on instead?
8. Do you have a rough budget range in mind, or is this the first time you're pricing something like this?
9. Who would need to be involved in saying yes to moving forward?
10. Is there anything about how your business runs that makes it unusual or more complex than a typical small business?
```

---

# How to Use

1. Use during the Consultation Call (RLOUT-007) or in a written discovery exchange before quoting.
2. Answers determine whether the standard $399 AI Workflow Starter Pack fits, or whether a custom quote is needed.
3. Do not skip questions 6 and 8 — urgency and budget signal are part of the STRAT-008 lead scoring model.
4. Record notes from these answers in `Lead_Tracker.csv` under Problem Observed.
