# Purpose

The reply to send when a prospect declines. Short, graceful, no pressure, no further follow-up.

---

# Template

```text
Hi [First Name] — totally understood, thanks for letting me know either way.

Wishing [Company Name] well, and if that ever changes, feel free to reach out any time.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this message is sent (in practice, this one rarely needs edits).
2. This closes the thread — do not send Follow-Up 1 or Follow-Up 2 (RLOUT-005/006) after a clear decline.
3. Mark the lead's status as "Declined" in the Lead Tracker / private lead record and close it out with the Conversation Summary template in `13_Client_Communications/`.
4. Do not ask "why not" or attempt to re-pitch in the same thread — respect the decision.
