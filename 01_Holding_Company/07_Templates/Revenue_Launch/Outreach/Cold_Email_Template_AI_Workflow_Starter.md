# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this email is sent to any real recipient.
2. Personalize every bracketed placeholder — never send a generic, unedited copy.
3. Do not include the PayPal payment link in this first message. Price and payment only come up once the recipient shows interest.
4. Keep tone honest and low-pressure — no fake urgency, no guaranteed results.
