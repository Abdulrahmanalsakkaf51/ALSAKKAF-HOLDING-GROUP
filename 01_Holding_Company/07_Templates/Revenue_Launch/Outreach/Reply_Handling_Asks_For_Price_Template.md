# Purpose

The reply to send when a prospect directly asks "how much does this cost?" This is the one scenario where naming the price in writing is expected and appropriate.

---

# Template

```text
Hi [First Name] — happy to share that directly.

The [AI Workflow Starter Pack / AI Agent Starter Pack] is $399 / $450 USD, delivered in 5-7 business days once scope is confirmed — a single fixed-scope engagement, not a subscription or open-ended consulting arrangement. [If pilot-eligible per the Founder Send Queue: mention the Founding Client pilot price instead/in addition, exactly as recorded there — do not offer a discount that isn't already approved.]

If anything about [Company Name]'s situation falls outside a standard single-workflow scope, the honest next step is a short call first rather than a quote in writing — happy to do either.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this message is sent.
2. State only the currently approved price(s) from `09_AI_Systems/02_Tools/Atlas_Runtime/atlas_config.json` (or the Founder Send Queue for that lead if a pilot price applies) — never invent or improvise a discount.
3. The PayPal payment link may be included only if the prospect has clearly confirmed they want to proceed — a price question alone is not automatic confirmation. When in doubt, ask "would you like me to send the payment link, or would a short call first be more useful?" rather than sending it unprompted.
4. Any request for a price outside the two approved offers goes to "Request Custom Quote" (sales@alsakkafsystems.com) — do not improvise a number.
5. Log the reply and this response in the Communications Inbox Register.
