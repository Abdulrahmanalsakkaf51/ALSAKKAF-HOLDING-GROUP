# Purpose

The reply to send when a prospect asks how their data is handled. This is an escalation category (see the Escalation Matrix in `01_Holding_Company/04_Operations/13_Client_Communications/`) — this template only covers what is safe to say while a specific, verified answer is prepared; it is not a substitute for a real data-handling policy statement.

---

# Template

```text
Hi [First Name] — good question, and I'd rather give you a precise answer than a generic one.

At a high level: we only ever work with the information you choose to share for the scoped engagement, we don't sell or share it, and any tools or accounts involved stay under your control, not ours. For the specifics that matter to your situation, let me put together a short, accurate answer and follow up separately rather than guess in this reply.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

---

# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this message is sent — this category always escalates to the Founder per the Escalation Matrix, never an automated or improvised reply.
2. Do not state a specific security certification, compliance standard, or data-retention guarantee unless it is independently true and Founder-confirmed in writing first.
3. Never promise data deletion timelines, encryption standards, or regulatory compliance (e.g., GDPR, ISO 27001) unless that claim has already been verified and approved — if unsure, leave it out.
4. Log the reply and this response in the Communications Inbox Register, and flag it in the Founder Approval Queue as a security/data question requiring a real follow-up answer.
