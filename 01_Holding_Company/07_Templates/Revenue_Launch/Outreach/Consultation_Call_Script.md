# Purpose

A short outline for a free intro consultation call with a prospective client.

---

# Script Outline

```text
1. Opening (1-2 min)
   - Thank them for the time.
   - Confirm how much time they have.
   - One-line reminder of who you are: "I'm Abdulrahman from ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP — AOS AI Services. I help small businesses turn repetitive manual work into simple AI-assisted workflows."

2. Discovery (5-8 min)
   - Ask the questions in Discovery_Questions_Template.md (RLOUT-008).
   - Listen more than you talk. Take notes.

3. Reflect the problem back (1-2 min)
   - Summarize what you heard in their words: "So it sounds like [specific problem] is costing you [time/money/stress]."

4. Present the offer, honestly (2-3 min)
   - If it's a clean fit: "This sounds like a good match for our AI Workflow Starter Pack — $399 USD. It includes [list the deliverables from STRAT-007]. Delivery is typically 5-7 business days."
   - If it's not a clean fit or the scope is bigger: "This looks like it needs a bit more than the standard package — I'd want to put together a custom quote rather than guess."

5. Close (1-2 min)
   - If interested in the standard pack: "If you'd like to go ahead, I can send you the payment link — it's a straightforward $399 USD PayPal link, and we get started as soon as it's paid."
   - If custom scope: "I'll put together a custom quote and send it over — no obligation."
   - If not ready: "No pressure at all — happy to follow up whenever works for you."
```

---

# How to Use

1. This call itself does not require CEO approval to have — but the offer/price presented must match the CEO-approved catalog (STRAT-007), and any custom quote requires CEO approval before it is sent, per STRAT-008's Pricing Approval Rule.
2. Only offer the PayPal payment link once the client has clearly said they want to proceed with the standard $399 package.
3. Never promise a specific result or guaranteed outcome on the call.
4. Log the outcome in `Client_Pipeline.csv` after the call.
