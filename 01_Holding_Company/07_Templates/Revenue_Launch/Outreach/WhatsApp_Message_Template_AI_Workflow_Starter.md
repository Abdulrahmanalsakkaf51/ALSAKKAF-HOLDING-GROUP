# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this message is sent to any real recipient.
2. **Higher intrusion risk than email/LinkedIn.** Use WhatsApp only for warm contacts — people you already know, a referral, or someone who has explicitly welcomed WhatsApp contact. Do not use for cold strangers.
3. Personalize every bracketed placeholder.
4. Do not include the PayPal payment link in this first message.
