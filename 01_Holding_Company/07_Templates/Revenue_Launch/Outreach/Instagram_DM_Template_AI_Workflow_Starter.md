# Purpose

A short, casual Instagram DM introducing the AI Workflow Starter Pack — for businesses with an active Instagram presence.

---

# Template

```text
Hey [First Name]! Love what you're doing at [Company Name] 🙌 I help small businesses simplify repetitive admin work with AI-assisted workflows — noticed [specific, genuine observation]. Open to a quick chat about it?
```

---

# How to Use

1. CEO approval is required before this message is sent to any real recipient.
2. Personalize every bracketed placeholder — genuine observation only, no flattery that isn't true.
3. Do not include the PayPal payment link in this first message.
4. Use sparingly — Instagram DMs to strangers carry higher spam-perception risk than email/LinkedIn.
