# Preamble

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP was founded on the belief that businesses should exist to solve meaningful problems and improve people's lives.

We believe that profit is essential for sustainability, but it is not the purpose of our existence. Profit enables us to continue building solutions, creating opportunities, and investing in future generations.

Every company within ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall operate according to the principles defined in this Constitution.

---

# Our Identity

We are a technology-enabled holding group that builds businesses dedicated to solving meaningful problems, improving communities, and creating lasting value.

Artificial Intelligence is one of our greatest tools, but our true purpose is to build solutions that make life better.

---

# Our Purpose

Our purpose is to identify meaningful problems, develop practical solutions, and continuously improve the lives of individuals, businesses, and communities.

We measure our success by the value we create before the profit we earn.

---

# Our Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted technology-enabled holding groups by building businesses that improve everyday life through innovation, responsible leadership, and continuous learning.

---

# Our Founding Principles

## 1. Purpose Before Profit

Profit is the fuel that allows us to continue solving problems.

It is never the reason we exist.

---

## 2. Integrity Above Convenience

We never knowingly exploit, deceive, or unfairly harm people for financial gain.

---

## 3. Solve Real Problems

Every project must solve a meaningful problem.

If it does not improve someone's life, we question why we are building it.

---

## 4. Continuous Learning

Learning never stops.

Every employee, manager, and leader remains a student.

---

## 5. Excellence Through Systems

We build systems, not chaos.

Knowledge is documented.

Processes are improved.

Quality is intentional.

---

## 6. Courage

We are not afraid of failure.

We are afraid of failing to learn.

---

## 7. Respect

We treat customers, employees, suppliers, partners, and competitors with professionalism, fairness, and respect.

---

## 8. Long-Term Thinking

We make decisions that strengthen the company for decades, not merely for the next quarter.

---

# What We Will Never Become

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP will never intentionally participate in businesses that depend on exploitation, deception, or harm to society.

As of Version 1.0, this includes industries such as:

- Gambling
- Tobacco
- Adult entertainment
- Predatory lending

Future leadership may review new industries using the principles of this Constitution, but any decision must remain consistent with our purpose and values.

---

# Decision Framework

Before approving any new project, business, product, or partnership, we ask:

1. Does it solve a real problem?
2. Does it genuinely improve people's lives?
3. Does it align with our values?
4. Can we deliver it responsibly?
5. Would we still be proud of this decision twenty years from now?

If the answer to any of these questions is "No", we reconsider the decision.

---

# Our Responsibility

We hope our work inspires others to create solutions of their own.

Our success is not measured by how many companies we own, but by how many positive changes those companies create.

---

# Commitment

This Constitution is the highest governing document of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

Every policy, strategy, company, AI system, and future decision should remain consistent with the principles established here.
