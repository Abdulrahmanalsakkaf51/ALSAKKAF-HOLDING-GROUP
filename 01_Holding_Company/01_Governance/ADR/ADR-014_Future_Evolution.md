# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall treat AOS as a living institutional system that improves over time.

AOS may evolve continuously, but it must remain aligned with the Company Constitution.

---

# Reason

Technology, markets, people, and problems change over time.

The institution must be able to adapt without losing its purpose, values, knowledge, or identity.

---

# Consequences

AOS shall use versioning, review cycles, change control, archiving, and AOS University updates to manage evolution.

Major changes to architecture should be documented through ADRs.

Constitutional changes require Founder / CEO approval.
