# Decision

The primary architectural document of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall be known as **Digital DNA**.

The operating system described by this document is known as **AOS — ALSAKKAF Operating System**.

---

# Reason

A traditional blueprint describes how something is initially built.

DNA continuously guides growth throughout the life of an organism.

The term Digital DNA better reflects the long-term vision of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP as a living institution that learns, grows, and evolves.

---

# Consequences

All future architectural standards shall reference the Digital DNA.

The Digital DNA becomes the master architectural record of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.
