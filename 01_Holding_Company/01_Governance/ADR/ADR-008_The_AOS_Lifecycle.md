# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use **The AOS Lifecycle** as the standard path for transforming ideas, problems, and opportunities into structured solutions.

The lifecycle is:

```text
Dream
  ↓
Question
  ↓
Problem
  ↓
Research
  ↓
Architecture
  ↓
Decision
  ↓
Project
  ↓
Prototype
  ↓
Validation
  ↓
Implementation
  ↓
Solution
  ↓
Feedback
  ↓
Knowledge
  ↓
AOS Improves
```
