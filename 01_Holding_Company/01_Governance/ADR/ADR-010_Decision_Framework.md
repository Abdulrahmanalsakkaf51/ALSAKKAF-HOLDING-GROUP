# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a structured Decision Framework inside AOS.

Every major decision must be evaluated using core decision questions, constitutional alignment, responsibility, and long-term impact.

---

# Reason

The institution is built on many ideas.

Without a decision framework, ideas may be approved too quickly, rejected too emotionally, or executed without proper understanding.

The Decision Framework protects ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP from random action and helps ensure that decisions are ethical, useful, responsible, and aligned with the institution’s purpose.

---

# Consequences

Major decisions must be documented.

Architectural decisions require ADRs.

Strategic and constitutional decisions require CEO approval.

Partners may support decision-making, but final accountability remains with human leadership.
