# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall adopt the rule:

> Nothing becomes part of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP until it is documented, approved, and traceable.

A Knowledge Register shall be created to index institutional knowledge.

---

# Reason

Approved knowledge that is not documented may be forgotten, duplicated, or misunderstood.

The Knowledge Register ensures that approved concepts can be found, reviewed, and updated.

---

# Consequences

Every approved institutional concept should be documented and indexed.

Future Partners, including a possible knowledge Partner called The Librarian, may use the Knowledge Register to help collaborators locate information.
