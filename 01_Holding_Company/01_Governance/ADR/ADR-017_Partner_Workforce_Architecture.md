# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a Partner Workforce Architecture to define how Partners are structured across the holding company and future companies.

The architecture shall support Partner roles such as Worker Partners, Supervisor Partners, Manager Partners, Company Lead Partners, Reporting Partners, and Knowledge Partners.

A future company may use a 30-Partner Company Cell as a target structure, but new companies should start smaller and scale gradually.

---

# Reason

The company vision includes multiple businesses supported by AI Partners.

Without workforce architecture, Partners may become duplicated, uncontrolled, unclear, or difficult to manage.

The Partner Workforce Architecture provides structure, reporting lines, authority boundaries, and a scalable model for Partner-powered companies.

---

# Consequences

Future companies should define their Partner structure before scaling.

Partner roles must include purpose, authority level, reporting line, knowledge sources, restrictions, and quality controls.

Human accountability remains required.

The 30-Partner Company Cell may be used as a reference model, not as a mandatory starting point.
