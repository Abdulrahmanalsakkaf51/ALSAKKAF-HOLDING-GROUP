# Decision

The Mind shall be defined as both the physical and digital center of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

The Physical Mind refers to the future headquarters.

The Digital Mind refers to AOS, the systems, records, Partners, dashboards, knowledge, and processes that allow the institution to operate before and after a physical headquarters exists.

---

# Reason

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP may begin without a physical building, but it should still operate with structure, intelligence, culture, and coordination.

By defining The Mind as both physical and digital, the institution can begin building its operating system immediately while preserving the long-term vision of a future headquarters.

---

# Consequences

The Mind shall be referenced in both physical design and digital architecture.

AOS is considered the Digital Mind of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

Future systems, Partners, dashboards, and knowledge tools should support the development of The Mind.
