# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall design The Librarian as the first official Partner profile.

The Librarian shall serve as the Knowledge Partner responsible for helping users find, understand, and reference approved institutional knowledge inside AOS.

---

# Reason

The company vision includes many Partners across multiple future companies.

Before many Partners are built, the institution needs a reliable way to find and use approved knowledge.

The Librarian supports the Knowledge System by connecting users and future Partners to the correct documents, decisions, lessons, and records.

---

# Consequences

The Librarian shall be designed before more advanced Partners.

The Librarian must distinguish between approved knowledge, proposed knowledge, missing knowledge, and knowledge that needs review.

The Librarian may assist, recommend, and prepare knowledge summaries, but it may not approve decisions or replace human accountability.
