# Decision

The headquarters of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall be known as **The Mind**.

---

# Reason

The Mind represents more than a physical headquarters.

It is the central intelligence, cultural heart, and coordination center of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

---

# Consequences

The term "The Mind" shall be used in institutional language, architecture documents, and future campus design.
