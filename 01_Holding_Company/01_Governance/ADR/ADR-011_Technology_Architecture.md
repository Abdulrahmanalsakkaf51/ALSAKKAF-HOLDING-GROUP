# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall treat technology as a supporting layer of AOS, not as AOS itself.

AOS may use many tools over time, but the institution’s architecture, knowledge system, decision framework, and principles must remain stable.

---

# Reason

Tools change quickly.

Institutions must last longer than tools.

By separating AOS from any single software application, ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP avoids becoming dependent on one platform or technology trend.

---

# Consequences

Technology decisions must be evaluated before adoption.

Every major tool should have a clear purpose, owner, data location, security approach, and relationship to AOS.

Partners may support collaborators, but human accountability remains required.
