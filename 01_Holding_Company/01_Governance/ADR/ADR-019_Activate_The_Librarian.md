# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall activate The Librarian as the first prompt-based Partner inside AOS.

The Librarian shall operate as an active Knowledge Partner with Authority Level 1–3.

---

# Reason

The Librarian passed foundation testing.

The test results confirmed that The Librarian can identify correct sources, distinguish approved knowledge from designed or proposed knowledge, avoid inventing approvals, and recommend useful next actions.

The Librarian is needed as the first active Partner because future Partners and collaborators require reliable access to institutional knowledge.

---

# Consequences

The Librarian may now be used actively for AOS knowledge support.

The Librarian remains limited to assisting, recommending, and preparing knowledge summaries.

The Librarian may not approve decisions, change official documents by itself, or replace human accountability.

The Partner Registry shall be updated to show The Librarian as Active.
