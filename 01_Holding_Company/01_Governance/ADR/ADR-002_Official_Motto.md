# Decision

The official motto of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP is:

> "We build organizations where people and artificial intelligence work together to solve meaningful problems, improve lives, and inspire future generations."

---

# Reason

This motto reflects the institution's purpose, its belief in human and AI collaboration, and its long-term commitment to solving meaningful problems.

---

# Consequences

The motto should appear in major identity documents, including LANG-001, DNA-001, and future onboarding materials.
