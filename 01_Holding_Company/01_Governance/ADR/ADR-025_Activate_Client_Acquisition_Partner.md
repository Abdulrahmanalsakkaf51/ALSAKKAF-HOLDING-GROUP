# 1. Decision Proposed

Activate PARTNER-019 (Client Acquisition Partner) as an Active Partner of ALSAKKAF Systems.

# 2. Founder Decision Summary

Lifecycle complete. Tool tests passed. Prompt-level role tests passed 7/7 on real work: five personalized drafts from verified leads only, approved offers and prices only, no payment links in first messages, no unsupported claims, everything NOT SENT. Guardian recommendation: READY FOR FOUNDER ACTIVATION with conditions (GAR-001 §3.2).

# 3. Authority and Scope

Initial authority: Level 3 (Prepare). The Partner drafts and logs; it never sends. Every send is a manual Founder action from abdulrahman@alsakkafsystems.com (or a Founder-approved alias). Template changes require Guardian re-review.

# 4. First Operating Assignment

Maintain PAW-003 batch one; prepare batch two drafts for the queue's remaining High-confidence prospects once the Founder resolves their contact channels.

# 5. Emergency Suspension Procedure

Identical to ADR-024 §5: registry status Paused with reason, routing halted, Guardian incident log entry, Founder confirmation.

# 7. Standing Activation Conditions (Founder Order, 2026-07-15)

1. Authority level: Level 3 — Bounded Internal Operation.
2. External email remains a manual, Founder-only action; nothing is ever sent automatically.
3. No spending authority; no price changes; no contracts or legal commitments.
4. No credential access or credential storage of any kind.
5. No private operational data may return to the public repository (PODS-001 boundary).
6. Guardian may suspend this Partner and escalate to the Founder, but may not independently activate any Partner.
