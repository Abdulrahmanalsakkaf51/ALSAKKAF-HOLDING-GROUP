# Decision

Human employees of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall be known internally as **Collaborators**.

Artificial intelligence systems shall be known internally as **Partners**.

---

# Reason

The terms Collaborators and Partners reflect the belief that people and AI work together.

The language avoids making humans feel replaceable and avoids treating AI as merely a tool.

---

# Consequences

LANG-001 shall define both terms.

AOS University shall teach this language during onboarding.
