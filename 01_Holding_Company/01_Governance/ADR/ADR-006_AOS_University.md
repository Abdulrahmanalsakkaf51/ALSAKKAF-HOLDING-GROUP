# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall establish **AOS University** as the internal learning system for the institution.

---

# Reason

AOS University will teach collaborators how the institution works, how AOS operates, how decisions are made, and how people and Partners work together.

It will also preserve lessons learned during the building of AOS.

---

# Consequences

AOS University shall contain structured lessons, onboarding materials, future certifications, and training for collaborators, leaders, managers, and Partners.
