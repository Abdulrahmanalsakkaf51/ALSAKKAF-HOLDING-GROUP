# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a Partner Registry as the official control record for Partners inside AOS.

The Partner Registry shall track proposed, designed, testing, approved, active, paused, retired, and rejected Partners.

---

# Reason

The company vision includes many Partners across multiple companies and ecosystems.

Without a registry, Partners may become duplicated, unclear, uncontrolled, or misaligned with AOS.

The Partner Registry creates structure, traceability, accountability, and governance for the Partner-powered company model.

---

# Consequences

No Partner becomes official until it is documented in the Partner Registry.

Each Partner must have an ID, purpose, type, owner, authority level, status, and related documents.

The first recommended Partner to design and test is The Librarian.

Future companies under ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP may have their own Partner structures connected to the main Partner Registry.
