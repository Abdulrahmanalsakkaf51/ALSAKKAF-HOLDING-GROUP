# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a Partner Operating Model to define how AI systems, automation workflows, digital assistants, and intelligent tools support AOS.

The company shall recognize its current stage as a Founder-Led Partner-Powered Stage.

---

# Reason

Before ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP has employees, offices, revenue, or a physical headquarters, it still needs structure.

Partners allow the Founder to extend capacity while AOS preserves governance, documentation, knowledge, and accountability.

A Partner Operating Model is required to prevent random AI usage and to ensure that Partners remain aligned with the Constitution, Digital DNA, and human responsibility.

---

# Consequences

Partners must have documented purpose, authority level, knowledge sources, restrictions, and review process.

The Partner Registry shall become the official list of approved, proposed, and retired Partners.

The first recommended Partner is The Librarian.

Partners may support decisions, but final accountability remains with human leadership.
