# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a staged Growth Model inside AOS.

The institution will grow through disciplined stages rather than launching many businesses at once.

---

# Reason

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP is built on many ideas.

Without a growth model, too many ideas may compete for time, money, and attention.

The Growth Model protects the institution by ensuring that expansion happens only when the foundation, knowledge, operations, and systems are ready.

---

# Consequences

The institution should focus on building one strong foundation before expansion.

The first successful business should become a learning model for future businesses.

Every major growth stage must strengthen AOS.

Growth must remain aligned with the Constitution, Digital DNA, and long-term purpose.
