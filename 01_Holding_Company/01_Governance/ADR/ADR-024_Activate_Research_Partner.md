# 1. Decision Proposed

Activate PARTNER-004 (Research Partner, Market Intelligence focus) as an Active Partner of ALSAKKAF Systems.

# 2. Founder Decision Summary

Lifecycle complete: request, profile, prompt, skill, activation checklist. Tool tests passed (13/13 pod suite). Prompt-level role tests passed 7/7 on real work: 10 real prospects verified through official websites, 8 unsuitable candidates rejected with reasons, no facts invented. Guardian recommendation: READY FOR FOUNDER ACTIVATION (GAR-001 §3.1).

# 3. Authority and Scope

Initial authority: Level 3 (Prepare). Approved network scope: READ-ONLY verification of public business websites for lead verification under active revenue projects. No posting, no accounts, no scraping beyond page reads, no contact with prospects.

# 4. First Operating Assignment

Verify the second prospect batch (targets from MKTI-001 markets) and re-verify LEAD-001..010 details flagged "verify manually" in PAW-001.

# 5. Emergency Suspension Procedure

The Founder (or Guardian recommendation + Founder confirmation) suspends by: (1) setting Partner Registry status to Paused with date and reason, (2) halting all routing to the role in POD-001 operations, (3) logging the incident in the Guardian incident record. Suspension is immediate; investigation follows.
