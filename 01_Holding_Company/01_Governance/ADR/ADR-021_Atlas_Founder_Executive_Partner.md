# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall design Atlas as PARTNER-002 — Founder Executive Partner.

Atlas shall support the Founder with daily briefings, priorities, projects, follow-ups, documents, data, and Partner coordination.

---

# Reason

The Founder requires a command Partner that can help manage the growing complexity of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

The Librarian helps find institutional knowledge.

Atlas will help the Founder organize action, priorities, projects, data, communication, and future Partner coordination.

Atlas is required to move the company from knowledge management toward Founder command support.

---

# Consequences

Atlas shall be listed in the Partner Registry as Designed.

Atlas shall begin with Authority Level 1–3.

Atlas may assist, recommend, and prepare.

Atlas may not approve major decisions, spend money, send official messages, delete files, change official records, or control the computer without approval.

The first recommended Atlas capability is Atlas Daily Briefing.
