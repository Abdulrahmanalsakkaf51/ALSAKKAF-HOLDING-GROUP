# Decision

The Mind shall include the following approved areas:

| Area | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| The Core | CEO, executive leadership, strategic decisions |
| Innovation Lab | Research, experimentation, prototypes |
| Future Builders Lab | Programs for children, students, internships, and future innovators |
| The Forge | Product development, engineering, software, and AI systems |
| Harmony | Human wellbeing, employee experience, culture, and learning |
| The Nexus | Meetings, collaboration, and cross-company projects |
| The Observatory | Company dashboards, analytics, AI monitoring, and business intelligence |
| The Vault | Company knowledge, legal records, intellectual property, patents, research, and history |
| The Launchpad | Where new ideas begin and are validated |
| The Pulse | Organizational health, morale, culture, performance, and wellbeing monitoring |

---

# Reason

These areas give The Mind a clear internal structure and create a shared institutional language.

---

# Consequences

Future designs of The Mind, AOS University, and Digital DNA should reference these areas consistently.
