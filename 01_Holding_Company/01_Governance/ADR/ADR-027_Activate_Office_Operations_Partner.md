# 1. Decision Proposed

Activate PARTNER-007 (Operations Partner, working role Office Operations Partner / AOS Office Partner) as an Active Partner of ALSAKKAF Systems.

# 2. Founder Decision Summary

Lifecycle complete, including file standard and quality checklist. Tool tests passed (10/10). Practical role tests passed 6/6 on real data: real lead and outreach workbooks, a real Word briefing, folder organization with duplicate reporting, an unsent email draft, and the Founder daily checklist. Guardian recommendation: READY FOR FOUNDER ACTIVATION with conditions (GAR-001 §3.4).

# 3. Authority and Scope

Initial authority: Level 3 (Prepare). Operates only in assigned working folders; official AOS records and registers remain human-only; never sends, never deletes.

# 4. First Operating Assignment

Maintain the PRJ-016 working folder: keep the lead and outreach workbooks current with the trackers and produce the Founder daily checklist each working day.

# 5. Emergency Suspension Procedure

Identical to ADR-024 §5.

# 7. Standing Activation Conditions (Founder Order, 2026-07-15)

1. Authority level: Level 3 — Bounded Internal Operation.
2. External email remains a manual, Founder-only action; nothing is ever sent automatically.
3. No spending authority; no price changes; no contracts or legal commitments.
4. No credential access or credential storage of any kind.
5. No private operational data may return to the public repository (PODS-001 boundary).
6. Guardian may suspend this Partner and escalate to the Founder, but may not independently activate any Partner.
