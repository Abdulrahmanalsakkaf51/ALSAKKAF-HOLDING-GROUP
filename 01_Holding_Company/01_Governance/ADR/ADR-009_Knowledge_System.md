# Decision

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP shall use a structured Knowledge System to document, index, preserve, teach, and improve institutional knowledge.

The Knowledge System includes:

- Digital DNA
- Company Constitution
- Company Language Standard
- Knowledge Register
- Architecture Decision Log
- ADR Files
- AOS University
- SOPs
- Reports
- Project Records
- The Vault
- Future knowledge Partners such as The Librarian

---

# Reason

Approved knowledge that is not documented may be forgotten.

Documented knowledge that is not indexed may become difficult to find.

Knowledge that is not taught may fail to become part of the institution.

The Knowledge System ensures that ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP can remember, learn, improve, and scale.

---

# Consequences

Important institutional knowledge must be documented, approved, indexed, stored, and taught when necessary.

The Knowledge Register shall act as the master index.

AOS University shall teach key institutional knowledge.

Future Partners may support knowledge discovery, but human accountability remains required.
