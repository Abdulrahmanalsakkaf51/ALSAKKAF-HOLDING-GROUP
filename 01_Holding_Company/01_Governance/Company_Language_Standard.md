# Language Principles

The language of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP exists to create clarity, consistency, and culture.

Every official term should:

- Describe its purpose.
- Be easy to understand.
- Remain meaningful for decades.
- Encourage collaboration rather than hierarchy.
- Reflect the values of the institution.

Whenever possible, institutional language should replace generic corporate terminology.

---

# Naming Convention

Official institutional names should follow these principles:

- Be meaningful rather than fashionable.
- Describe a role or purpose.
- Be consistent across the organization.
- Avoid unnecessary abbreviations.
- Support long-term institutional growth.

Examples:

Headquarters → The Mind

Employee → Collaborator

Artificial Intelligence → Partner

Meeting Space → The Nexus
