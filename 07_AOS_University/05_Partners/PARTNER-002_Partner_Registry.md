# Objective

Understand what the Partner Registry is and why it is important.

---

# What Is the Partner Registry?

The Partner Registry is the official list of Partners inside ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

It tracks Partners that are:

- Proposed
- Designed
- Testing
- Approved
- Active
- Paused
- Retired
- Rejected

No Partner should be considered official until it appears in the Partner Registry.

---

# Why the Registry Matters

Without a registry, Partners may become confusing, duplicated, uncontrolled, or unsafe.

The registry helps AOS know:

- Which Partners exist
- What each Partner does
- Who owns each Partner
- What authority each Partner has
- What knowledge sources each Partner uses
- What status each Partner has

---

# First Partner Priority

The first Partner to design and test should be The Librarian.

The Librarian helps users find approved institutional knowledge inside AOS.

This Partner should be built before more advanced Partners because future Partners need reliable knowledge access.

---

# Partner Authority

At the foundation stage, Partners may operate only from Level 0 to Level 3:

- Reference
- Assist
- Recommend
- Prepare

Autonomous execution is not approved.
