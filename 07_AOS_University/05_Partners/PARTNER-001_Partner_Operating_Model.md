# What Is a Partner?

A Partner is an AI system, automation workflow, digital assistant, or intelligent tool that supports the institution.

Partners may help with research, documentation, planning, analysis, drafting, searching knowledge, and supporting decisions.

Partners are not employees.

Partners are support systems inside AOS.

---

# Current Company Stage

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP is currently in the Founder-Led Partner-Powered Stage.

This means:

- The Founder provides vision and accountability.
- AOS provides structure and memory.
- Partners support execution and thinking.
- Human leadership remains responsible.

---

# Human Accountability

Partners may assist, recommend, summarize, draft, and analyze.

Partners do not make final decisions.

Partners do not replace the CEO.

Partners do not replace human judgment.

---

# Partner Authority Levels

Partners may have different authority levels:

- Reference
- Assist
- Recommend
- Prepare
- Execute with approval
- Autonomous execution

At the foundation stage, autonomous execution is not approved.

---

# First Partner Priority

The first official Partner should be The Librarian.

The Librarian helps people find approved institutional knowledge inside AOS.
