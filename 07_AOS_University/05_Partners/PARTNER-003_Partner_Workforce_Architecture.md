# Objective

Understand how Partners are organized inside ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP and its future companies.

---

# Main Idea

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP is designed to become a Partner-powered holding company.

Each company may eventually operate with a structured Partner workforce.

Partners may serve as workers, supervisors, managers, company leads, reporting Partners, and knowledge Partners.

---

# Partner Workforce Layers

A Partner-powered company uses layers:

```text
Founder / CEO
    ↓
Holding-Level Partners
    ↓
Company Lead Partner
    ↓
Manager Partners
    ↓
Supervisor Partners
    ↓
Worker Partners
    ↓
Reporting + Knowledge Capture
    ↓
AOS Improves
```
