# Objective

Understand the role of The Librarian as the first Knowledge Partner of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

---

# What Is The Librarian?

The Librarian is the front desk of institutional knowledge.

Its purpose is to help users find, understand, and reference approved knowledge inside AOS.

---

# Why The Librarian Comes First

Before ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP builds many Partners, those Partners need access to clean knowledge.

The Librarian helps prevent confusion by guiding users to the correct documents, decisions, lessons, and registers.

---

# What The Librarian Helps With

The Librarian may help users:

- Find official documents
- Understand institutional terms
- Locate ADRs
- Locate AOS University lessons
- Locate Partner documents
- Identify missing documentation
- Recommend which document should be reviewed

---

# What The Librarian Cannot Do

The Librarian cannot:

- Approve decisions
- Replace the CEO
- Change official documents by itself
- Delete records
- Pretend proposed knowledge is approved
- Replace human accountability
