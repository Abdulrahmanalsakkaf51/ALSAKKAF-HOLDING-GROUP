# Objective

Understand why ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP documents, indexes, and preserves institutional knowledge.

---

# Why Institutional Knowledge Matters

A company can forget.

An institution must remember.

Ideas, decisions, lessons, systems, and principles become valuable only when they are preserved and made discoverable.

If knowledge remains only in a person's memory or a chat conversation, it can be lost.

If knowledge is documented, approved, indexed, and versioned, it becomes part of the institution.

---

# The Knowledge Rule

> If it is approved, it must be documented.
>
> If it is documented, it must be discoverable.

---

# The Knowledge Register

The Knowledge Register is the master index of institutional knowledge.

It does not replace documents.

It helps people find them.

---
