# AOS University

AOS University is the internal learning system of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

It exists to teach collaborators how the institution works, how AOS operates, how knowledge is preserved, and how people and Partners work together.

---

# Purpose

AOS University helps ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP preserve knowledge, train collaborators, develop leaders, and teach the institutional culture.

Every approved lesson should help future collaborators understand, apply, or improve AOS.
