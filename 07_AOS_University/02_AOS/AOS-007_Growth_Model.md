# Objective

Understand how ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP grows through AOS without becoming chaotic.

---

# Why Growth Needs a Model

Growth can be dangerous if it is not controlled.

A company with many ideas but no system can become distracted, disorganized, and financially weak.

The AOS Growth Model helps ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP grow through discipline, documentation, validation, and learning.

---

# Main Principle

One strong foundation can support many companies.

Many weak foundations create chaos.

---

# Growth Stages

ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP grows through stages:

1. Founder Stage
2. AOS Foundation Stage
3. First Project Stage
4. First Business Stage
5. Shared Services Stage
6. Multi-Business Stage
7. Institution Stage

Each stage must strengthen AOS before moving to the next stage.

---

# Growth Rule

Do not launch many businesses at once.

Build one successful business first.

Document it.

Learn from it.

Then use that knowledge to build the next one better.

---

# Revenue

Revenue is important, but revenue is not the purpose.

Revenue is fuel.

Purpose is direction.
