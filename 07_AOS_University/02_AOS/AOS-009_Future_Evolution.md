# Objective

Understand how AOS improves over time while protecting the identity of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

---

# Main Idea

AOS is a living institutional system.

It should not remain frozen.

It should learn, improve, and evolve.

However, AOS must never evolve away from the Constitution.

---

# What Should Stay Protected

Some parts of ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP should only change with great care:

- Purpose
- Constitutional principles
- Ethical commitments
- Human accountability
- Responsible use of Partners
- Documentation-first culture
- Commitment to solving meaningful problems

---

# What Should Improve

Other parts should improve continuously:

- SOPs
- Templates
- Lessons
- Partner capabilities
- Dashboards
- Reports
- Project methods
- Technology tools
- Knowledge organization

---

# Versioning

Important documents should use version numbers.

Examples:

- Digital DNA v1.0
- Digital DNA v1.1
- Digital DNA v2.0

Versioning helps the institution remember how it changed.

---

# Archive

Important knowledge should not be deleted casually.

If something is no longer used, it should be retired or archived.

The institution should remember what it tried, what worked, what failed, and why.
