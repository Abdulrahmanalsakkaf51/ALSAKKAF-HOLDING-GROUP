# Objective

Understand how ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP is organized through AOS architecture and ecosystems.

---

# What Architecture Means

Architecture explains how the institution is organized.

It is not only about folders, buildings, or documents.

Architecture explains how people, Partners, projects, knowledge, decisions, and businesses connect together.

---

# Network Architecture

AOS is designed as a network, not a traditional hierarchy.

Traditional companies often separate work into departments.

AOS connects ecosystems through projects, knowledge, collaborators, Partners, and continuous feedback.

---

# What Ecosystems Mean

An ecosystem is a connected area of capability inside ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

An ecosystem combines:

- Collaborators
- Partners
- Knowledge
- Projects
- Tools
- Processes
- Decisions
- Continuous improvement

---

# Why Ecosystems Matter

Ecosystems prevent the institution from becoming isolated into disconnected departments.

They allow knowledge and execution to move across the organization while still staying aligned with the Constitution and Digital DNA.

---

# Example

A new business idea may involve:

- Strategy Ecosystem
- Innovation Ecosystem
- Projects Ecosystem
- Technology Ecosystem
- Partner Ecosystem
- Knowledge Ecosystem
- Business Ecosystem

This prevents ideas from being built randomly.

Ideas are evaluated, documented, developed, tested, and improved through AOS.
