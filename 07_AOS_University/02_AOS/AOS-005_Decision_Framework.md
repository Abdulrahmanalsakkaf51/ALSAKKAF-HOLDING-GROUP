# Objective

Understand how ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP makes important decisions through AOS.

---

# Why Decisions Need a Framework

AOS exists to prevent random action.

Without a decision framework, the institution may approve ideas because they are exciting, profitable, popular, or urgent.

The Decision Framework protects ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP by making sure decisions are aligned with purpose, values, responsibility, and long-term value.

---

# Core Decision Questions

Before approving a major idea, project, product, company, partnership, or system, AOS asks:

1. What real problem does this solve?
2. Who benefits from this solution?
3. Can we deliver it responsibly and sustainably?

---

# Constitutional Check

AOS also checks decisions against the Company Constitution.

A decision must be ethical, valuable, aligned with purpose, respectful to people and society, and something the institution can remain proud of in the future.

---

# Decision Levels

AOS separates decisions into levels:

- Daily decisions
- Operational decisions
- Architectural decisions
- Strategic decisions
- Constitutional decisions

The more important the decision, the more carefully it must be reviewed and documented.

---

# Role of Partners

Partners may support decision-making through research, analysis, summaries, comparisons, and risk identification.

Partners support decisions.

They do not own final accountability.

Human leadership remains responsible.
