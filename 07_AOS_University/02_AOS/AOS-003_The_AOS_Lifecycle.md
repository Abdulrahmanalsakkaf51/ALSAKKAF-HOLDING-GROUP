# What Is the AOS Lifecycle?

The AOS Lifecycle is the standard path used to transform a dream or idea into a structured solution.

It prevents ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP from building randomly.

Instead of jumping from idea to execution, AOS moves through a clear process.

---
