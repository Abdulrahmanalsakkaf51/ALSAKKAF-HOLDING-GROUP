# Objective

Understand why professional documentation is divided into chapters.

---

# Why Chapters Exist

Most people write documents like essays.

Professional organizations write documents like systems.

Every chapter should answer one primary question.

This allows readers to quickly find information without reading the entire document.

---

# Example

Question:

Why does AOS exist?

Answer:

Digital DNA

Chapter 1 — Philosophy

---

Question:

How is AOS organized?

Answer:

Digital DNA

Chapter 2 — Architecture

---

Question:

How is knowledge stored?

Answer:

Digital DNA

Knowledge System
