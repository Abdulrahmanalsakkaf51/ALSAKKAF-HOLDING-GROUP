# 1. Which Logo to Use

| Context | Logo |
|---------|------|
| Holding group documents, governance, long-term vision material | ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP lockup |
| Public website, proposals, invoices, client delivery, email signatures | ALSAKKAF Systems lockup |
| Service marketing, Partner Playground, AI service material | AOS AI Services lockup |
| Small spaces (favicons, avatars, watermarks) | Mark only |

On white or light backgrounds use the standard files. On dark backgrounds use the `-light` files.

---

# 2. Clear Space

Keep clear space around the logo equal to the height of the crossbar on every side. Do not place the logo against busy imagery without a solid or softly-blurred backing area.

---

# 3. Minimum Sizes

| Asset | Minimum |
|-------|---------|
| Full lockup | 120 px wide (digital) |
| Mark only | 20 px wide |

Below these sizes, use the mark only.

---

# 4. Color Rules

1. Never recolor the mark outside the approved palette (BRAND-001 Section 4).
2. Violet is reserved for AOS AI Services material only.
3. One-color fallback: all-navy on light backgrounds, all-white on dark backgrounds.
4. Never place the navy mark on a navy or dark background — use the light variant.

---

# 5. Do Not

- Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the logo.
- Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or glow effects.
- Do not rebuild the A with different geometry or fonts.
- Do not use raster AI-generated logo art in place of these files.
- Do not combine the three brand levels in one lockup.

---

# 6. Brand Naming

| Level | Correct written form |
|-------|----------------------|
| Master brand | ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP |
| Operating company | ALSAKKAF Systems |
| Service line | AOS AI Services |

"AOS" is written in capitals. "Systems" is capitalized but not all-caps in prose.
