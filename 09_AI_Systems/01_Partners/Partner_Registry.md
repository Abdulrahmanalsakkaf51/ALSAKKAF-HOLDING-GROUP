# 1. Purpose

The Partner Registry is the official list of Partners inside ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP.

It tracks proposed, approved, active, paused, retired, and future Partners.

The registry helps AOS understand:

- Which Partners exist
- What each Partner does
- Who owns each Partner
- What authority each Partner has
- What knowledge sources each Partner may use
- Which company or ecosystem the Partner supports
- Whether the Partner is proposed, approved, active, or retired

No Partner should be considered official until it appears in this registry.

---

# 2. Partner Status Definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Proposed | Partner idea has been identified but not built |
| Designed | Partner purpose and rules have been documented |
| Testing | Partner is being tested |
| Approved | Partner has been approved for use |
| Active | Partner is currently being used |
| Paused | Partner is temporarily not in use |
| Retired | Partner is no longer used but remains documented |
| Rejected | Partner idea was reviewed and not approved |

---

# 3. Partner Authority Levels

| Level | Name | Meaning |
|------|------|---------|
| Level 0 | Reference | Provides information only |
| Level 1 | Assist | Helps draft, summarize, organize, or explain |
| Level 2 | Recommend | Suggests options for human review |
| Level 3 | Prepare | Prepares documents, plans, or workflows for approval |
| Level 4 | Execute With Approval | Executes low-risk tasks only after human approval |
| Level 5 | Autonomous Execution | Not approved at the foundation stage |

At the foundation stage, Partners may only operate from **Level 0 to Level 3**.

Level 4 may be reviewed later.

Level 5 is not approved.

# 6. First Partner Priority

The first Partner to design and test should be:

## PARTNER-001 — The Librarian

The Librarian should be built first because all future Partners need access to clean institutional knowledge.

Before building many Partners, AOS must know how to find, explain, and reference its own knowledge.

The Librarian will become the front desk of institutional knowledge.

---

# 7. Future Company Partner Structure

Each future company under ALSAKKAF HOLDING GROUP may eventually have its own Partner structure.

A future company may include:

```text
Company Lead Partner
    ↓
Manager Partners
    ↓
Supervisor Partners
    ↓
Worker Partners
    ↓
Reporting Partner
    ↓
Knowledge Captured into AOS
```

The exact structure will depend on the business model.

A marketing company may need content, campaign, design, analytics, and client Partners.

A real estate company may need property research, listing, broker support, customer inquiry, booking, and pricing Partners.

Each company must have a clear Partner structure before scaling.

---

# 8. Partner Registry Rules

1. Every Partner must have a Partner ID.
2. Every Partner must have a clear purpose.
3. Every Partner must have an owner.
4. Every Partner must have an authority level.
5. Every Partner must have a status.
6. Every Partner must have related documents.
7. No Partner may become active without testing.
8. No Partner may exceed its approved authority level.
9. No Partner may replace human accountability.
10. Retired Partners must remain documented for institutional memory.

---

# 9. Partner Approval Requirements

Before a Partner moves from Proposed to Approved, it must have:

- Partner name
- Partner purpose
- Partner type
- Authority level
- Owner
- Knowledge sources
- Allowed tasks
- Restricted tasks
- Testing method
- Review cycle
- Human approval rules

---

# 10. Partner Registry Principle

> Partners become powerful only when they operate inside clear purpose, clear boundaries, and clear accountability.
