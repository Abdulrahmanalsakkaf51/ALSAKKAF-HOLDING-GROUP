# 1. Purpose

This template is used immediately before a Partner becomes active, to confirm every required stage of the Partner Creation Lifecycle (PCL-001) has actually been completed.

A Partner must not be marked "Active" in the Partner Registry until every item on this checklist is confirmed.

---

# 2. How to Use This Template

1. Copy this file into the Partner's working folder or attach it to the Partner Profile.
2. Confirm each item honestly — do not check an item that is incomplete.
3. Capture the Founder's approval decision at the end.
4. Only after this checklist is fully confirmed may the Partner Registry be updated to "Active."
