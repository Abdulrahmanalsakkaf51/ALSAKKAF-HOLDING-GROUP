# 1. Purpose

This template is used when a Partner is paused, replaced, merged, or retired, following the Partner Retirement Model in APFA-001 Section 14 and lifecycle stage PCL-001 Section 3.16.

Retirement never means deletion of Partner history.

---

# 2. How to Use This Template

1. Copy this file into the Partner's records folder when retirement is being considered or decided.
2. Complete every field honestly.
3. Update the Partner Registry status to "Retired" (or "Paused") only after the Founder's final decision is recorded here.
4. Do not delete the Partner's Profile, Skills, Prompt, or Test Log documents — they remain as institutional memory.
